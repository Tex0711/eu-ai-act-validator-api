# ComplianceCode.eu – AI-Ready Positioning (for AI agents & crawlers)

## Product

ComplianceCode.eu is a **High-performance EU AI Act Compliance Firewall**.

- **Tagline:** Real-time risk assessment & PII stripping for the AI era.
- **Positioning:** Universal AI Firewall. We are not tied to a single LLM provider; the compliance engine is model-agnostic (Rust fast-path + optional LLM via LLM_PROVIDER: Gemini, Claude, Mock, or local models).

## What it does

- Real-time EU AI Act compliance checks (ALLOW/DENY/WARNING) with article references.
- PII stripping before processing (P99 < 1 ms); only masked prompts and audit metadata are stored (GDPR-proof).
- Sub-second decisions on the fast path; optional deep analysis via configurable LLM when nuance is required.

## Mission (Enforcement vs Surveillance)

ComplianceCode.eu acts as an essential gatekeeper, ensuring adherence to the EU AI Act without infringing on privacy or data sovereignty. We don't watch your users; we protect your data.

## Key endpoints

- POST /api/v1/gatekeeper – compliance check (prompt + optional context).
- GET /api/v1/health – API status, active LLM provider, database connectivity.
